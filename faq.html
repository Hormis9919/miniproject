Frequently Asked Questions

How does this car price predictor work?

Our model uses machine learning algorithms, including Random Forest and Extra Trees Regression, to analyze historical car sales data and predict used car prices.

What details do I need to enter for a prediction?

You need to provide details such as make, model, year, mileage, fuel type, transmission, and location to get an accurate price estimate.

Is this prediction 100% accurate?

No prediction model is 100% accurate, but our AI-powered system provides a highly reliable estimate based on past trends and real-world data.

Is the tool free to use?

Yes, this tool is free to use. Simply enter your car details and get a price estimate instantly.
